Programs

Our program has a biblically based curriculum that provides children with an early foundation in the word of God and imparts wholesome Christian concepts. We desire to not only teach wholesome Christian values, but to introduce children and families to Jesus Christ and a personal relationship with Him.

Our program is designed to aid all children in their total development. This program combines perceptual skills, motor skills, and cognitive development curriculum, resulting in a nicely balanced day for the children. Emphasis is placed on helping all children acquire the learning skills they need for kindergarten and the rest of their lives.

A daily program provides constructive experiences for each child through planned activity, creative play, music, stories, art projects, and personal counsel. These activities are both group and individual in nature.
