PreKindergarten Program
(On My Way)

Our PreK program is developed with your child in mind. We infuse our accredited curriculum with Christian values to support a solid growth in all corners of development, including a spiritual foundation. Our teachers guide your child in learning social and emotional skills needed to participate in Kindergarten and beyond. We work on general and enriched educational development to help in the demanding educational requirements for Elementary school.

Kiddie Prep School is an authorized provider for the On My Way Pre-K program. This acceptance program provides vouchers for FREE pre-kindergarten classes for children who will be four by August 1st. More information about this assistance program can be found on BrightPoint's website. Don’t miss the deadline to apply for the program year. Apply now at www.onmywayprek.org or call 2-1-1 to request an application. Don't miss this opportunity for your child. Apply for an On My Way voucher now!!!

Kiddie Prep enrolls year round but don't delay. Call our office at (260) 485-7951. Hope to see you soon!
