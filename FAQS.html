FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  1. When is Kiddie Prep School Open?- Kiddie Prep School is open from Monday through Friday, between the hours of 6:15 am and 6:00 pm.
  2. Does Kiddie Prep School Close for Snow Days or Delays? -Kiddie Prep School will close when the city of Fort Wayne, Allen County declares a warning in travel conditions. This is a level red emergency where all of Allen County is closed down. We do not delay school either. In case of an emergency situation, either before Kiddie Prep School opens for the day or during the normal hours of operation, KPS will post a notice in the following areas: WAJI 95.1, WBCL 90.3, WLAB 88.3, Facebook, KPS website and/or mass text from Procare Technology.
  3. What Holidays are Observed by Kiddie Prep School? - Kiddie Prep School is closed on New Year's Day, Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, the day after Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day. Kiddie Prep School closes early on Good Friday and Christmas Eve. If the holiday falls on a Saturday, Kiddie Prep School will be closed on Friday. If the holiday fall on a Sunday, Kiddie Prep School will be closed on Monday.
  4. How Old Must My Child Be Before They Can Begin Kiddie Prep School? - Kiddie Prep School is licensed to care for children ages 2-12.
  5. What Documents Need to be Completed and Given to the Kiddie Prep Office, Prior to my Child's First Day? - Registration Papers (available in the Kiddie Prep School Office ONLY)  - Physical and Immunization Form (available in the Kiddie Prep School Office or under the "forms" tab) - State Food Form (available in the Kiddie Prep School Office or under the "forms" tab)  -Original Birth Certificate (The office staff will make a copy of the original and return the original to you.)
  6. Is There A Registration Fee? - There is a yearly registration fee for preschool of $50.00. This fee covers curriculum costs, as well as classroom supplies. The fee is charged prior to enrollment and every September 1st.
  7. When are Tuition Payments Due? Tuition is posted each Monday and will be batched from the account on file each Friday.  You have the entire week to pay using a different method other than the card on file, until Friday when payment will be pulled from the account.    Failure to pay tuiton fees for two consecutive weeks will cause the child to be disenrolled. Parents may pay for two or three weeks at a time, if paying in advance. 
  8. What are the Acceptable Methods of Payment? - Kiddie Prep School accepts all major credit cards, checks, money orders, or online bill payments. Please NO CASH. There is a service charge for any returned check.
  9. What do I need to Know About My Child Receiving Medications at Kiddie Prep School? - The giving or application of medication, providing dietary supplements, making special variation of diet, and carrying out medical procedure, shall be done only on the written order or prescription from a physician to the parents, or to the school with the knowledge of the parents.           Medications prescribed for an individual child shall be kept in the original container bearing the original pharmacy label showing the prescription number, date filled, physician's name, directions for use, and the child's name. When the medication is no longer needed, medication shall be returned to the parents or destroyed.         Parents need to fill out a "Medication Form" available under the "forms" tab or available in each classroom through the teacher. The completed form should be given to the classroom teacher along with the medication and dosing spoon.
  10. When Will My Child be Sent Home if He/She is ill? - Children will be sent home for the following conditions:  Temperature of 100 degrees or higher -Sore or inflamed throat -Congested nasal passages  -Excessive coughing  -Any undiagnosed rash or skin condition  -Pediculosis (head lice)  -Scalp ringworm  -Headache, nausea  -Any other sign of illness until diagnosed  -Each situation will be given individual attention.
  11. Can I Visit My Child at Kiddie Prep School? - Parents are always welcome, we have an open door policy. Parents may observe children using the classroom monitoring system in the office, if they wish to go unnoticed by their child
  12. I Want to Celebrate My Child's Birthday at School.  Can I Bring in Treats? - We love celebrating birthdays and special events. All food/treats MUST be store bought and contained in the original store container showing the ingredients. There are several children with severe peanut allergies and we want to keep all children safe. NO decorations please unless your child's teacher asks you to bring them in for celebration days
  13. How Does the Staff At Kiddie Prep School Discipline The Children in their Care?  - Child training and discipline is handled with kindness and understanding, but with firmness. Proverbs 12:1 says "If you love learning, you love the discipline that goes with it - how shortsighted to refuse correction." The use of corporal punishment is forbidden. Brief, supervised separation from the group may be used if needed. Attention is given to the needs of each individual child, while at the same time the child is encouraged to become a part of the group through group participation.  Kiddie Prep School staff is all trained in the practice of Conscious Discipline.
