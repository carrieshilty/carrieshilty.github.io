Welcome to Kiddie Prep School

Since 1968, we have been honored to serve Fort Wayne/Leo, IN area, shaping young minds! We are a Christian school that offers preschool curriculum and child care. We offer services for children aged 2-12 years old. Our programs address the three most common learning styles, which are audio, visual, kinesthetic learning. We are always looking at the latest research on development to ensure we are offering the latest techniques for learning. Our staff is dedicated to providing loving care, direction and do it all from a Christian perspective!

Our Goals

We treasure the time that we spend with your children, we provide a Christian child care environment where your child can thrive. Our goal is to have the premier child care center in Indiana. We are committed to providing top care, education and helping your child reach development goals. It is our love of children that drives everything that we do!

The Curriculum and the Care
We are accredited with the Association of Christian Schools International. We offer curriculum that helps young developing minds. Our program of care and education has a Level 4 rating under the Indiana Paths to Quality rating system. Level 4 is the top rating! We will:

  • Provide your child with a great start to education
  • Provide a physical fitness exercise plan that will help your child’s development 
  • Keep you abreast of their progress
  • Make you aware of any changes that we notice 


We will partner with you in providing the education and care that your child needs to develop and thrive. We are conveniently located, have years of experience and provide a Christian based learning and care environment! Contact us today to learn more about our programs and find out how we can complement your child’s education!
